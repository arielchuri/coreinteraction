# Interview

## Learning Objectives

This assignment addresses the following course learning outcomes:

- **LO1:** Use a basic vocabulary of interactive media to both give and respond to critique productively.
- **LO4:** Conceptualize a product, object, or experience for the web and realize it through coding.
- **LO7:** Research historic and current design precedents to contextualize your own work.

## Assignment Description

Partner up with one of your classmates. Interview your partner. Create a series of linked web pages that introduces your assigned partner to the class. Your project should consist of at least 10 individual pages, and should reveal at least 10 facts about your partner, other than their name.

Use the tags we learned in class:

- H1 - H6
- p
- ul, ol, li
- img
- a
- div

Also use the following:

- strong
- em

Explore 3 new HTML tags of your own choosing. Make your image tags _accessable_ to people with a vision disability.

## Assessable Tasks

- [ ] Conduct an interview with your partner and gather meaningful information
- [ ] Create at least 10 individual HTML pages
- [ ] Implement navigation between pages using HTML links
- [ ] Present at least 10 facts about your partner (excluding their name)
- [ ] Use basic HTML structure and semantic tags appropriately
- [ ] Ensure all pages are properly linked and navigable

## Due Date

Week 3, Feb 9: Show your work in progress in class
Week 4, Feb 23: Turn in a link to you site's github repository

## Deliverables

- A folder containing all HTML files for your interview project
- All files should be properly linked and navigable
- Submit via GitHub repository

## Evaluation Criteria

- **Technical execution:** Proper use of HTML and linking structure
- **Content:** Quality and depth of interview questions and presentation
- **Navigation:** Clear and functional navigation between pages
- **Creativity:** Inventive ways of presenting information
- **Completeness:** All required pages and facts are present
